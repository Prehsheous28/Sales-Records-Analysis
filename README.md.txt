# 🌍 GlobalMart Sales Records Analysis (Excel Dashboard Project)

## 📌 Project Overview

This Excel-based project analyzes global sales data from **GlobalMart Retail Solutions**, a multinational retailer aiming for expansive global growth. The objective is to uncover actionable insights across product categories, regions, and sales channels to support strategic decision-making and supply chain optimization.

The data originates from Kaggle and includes over 10 years of transaction records, enabling a comprehensive view of market performance and business operations across various regions and categories.

---

## 📊 Tools & Technologies

- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Pivot Tables
  - Charts & Visualizations
  - Formulas (SUMIFS, VLOOKUP, etc.)
  - Slicers & Filters
- **Data Source**: Kaggle
- **Case Study Context**: Provided by GlobalMart Retail Solutions

---

## 🧾 Dataset Description

| Column Name    | Description                                               |
|----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| Region         | Geographic area of the transaction                        |
| Country        | Country of the transaction                                |
| Item Type      | Product category (e.g., Baby Food, Office Supplies)       |
| Sales Channel  | Online or Offline                                         |
| Order Priority | Priority assigned to the order (Low, Medium, High, etc.)  |
| Order Date     | Date when the order was placed                            |
| Ship Date      | Date when the order was shipped                           |
| Units Sold     | Number of units sold                                      |
| Unit Price     | Sale price per unit                                       |
| Unit Cost      | Cost per unit                                             |

---

## 🎯 Key Business Questions Answered

- Which **regions and countries** generate the highest revenue?
- What **item types** are most profitable?
- How does **sales performance differ** between online and offline channels?
- What’s the impact of **order priority** on delivery time?
- Which **months and years** show seasonal sales trends?

---

## 📈 Sample Insights

> Replace with actual findings from your dashboard:
- The **Australia & Oceania** region had the highest average profit per order.
- **Cereal** and **Baby Food** were consistently top-performing categories.
- **Offline sales** showed higher revenue but lower margin than online sales.
- **High-priority orders** were often delayed, suggesting fulfillment challenges.
